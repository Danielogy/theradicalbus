Another Mutual Aid.

The radical bus will be a rolling lbrary with the intention of bringing books to young students and young people to South & Center Brooklyn. Anyone under the age of 27, whether they are currently in the school system or not, will be able to check out books from their closest neighbourhood stop.

Access to readings variety is one of the biggest hurdles to library access for many families. The Radical Bus will face this challange head-on by meeting people where they are.

The Radical Bus is commited to alleviating some of the financial burders that can interfere with library acces. This means that our bus team will have more flexibility with penalities for late return or damage than the staff at most of other libraries. Not to mention, any penalities will never be monetary at The Radical Bus!
